You can choose among four types of policies. Business as usual is exactly that—no policy TKTK. Choosing an optimized policy allows the computer to choose an emissions reduction plan that will maximize social utility. Or you can simulate a simple global carbon tax, or global climate treaty.

Be aware that optimized policies can take about ten seconds to run, depending on your input parameters—and a bit longer longer if you’re using a BEAM carbon cycle.
